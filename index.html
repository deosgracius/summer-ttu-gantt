About Summer Timeline

Summer Timeline is a shared project tracker for student project-lab groups. Each group registers, gets its own private passcode, and tracks its project independently — no group can see another's data.

It answers, at a glance: what needs building (every task), who owns it, when it happens and how far along it is (the weekly Gantt with completion % and hours), and what it costs (the labor + materials + overhead budget, actual vs. estimate).

Two views: the Timeline (tasks across sections over your class schedule, with a live progress dashboard and a NOW marker) and the Budget (auto-calculated cost model). It's shared & live — everyone in your group with the passcode sees the same data and edits sync within seconds, kept private by passcode + database-level security.
